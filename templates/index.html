Hello! I am Nic Howe Garcia. Currently a Front-End Developer for the growth team at DeSales Media Group.

I've been a computer enthusiast almost my entire life. Ever since being endlessly curious when it came to how the things I was downloading onto my computer were working. I had an early realization that everything I was using on the computer was made by someone; nothing had just come out of the blue the same way it had for me. That sparked something in me and I've been interested in software development ever since.

I graduated from New York University with my Bachelor's in Computer Science in December of 2018. This was a culmination of years of effort that started by self-teaching myself python in middle school. Primarily starting with both the artistic and technical sides of game development. This naturally took me to wanting to know how to build large and efficient applications. I've put a lot of work into learning how to squeeze performance out of the environment I develop for, from learning practices for developing on large scale server applications to desktop grade multicore processors including graphics processors.

As a front-end developer I take pride in knowing how the work I am doing is interacting with the browser and how I can develop to increase performance for the user, and make their experience with the website as smooth and fast as possible.

As a part of my NYU degree I also achieved minors in game design and creative writing. I never lost neither my passion when it comes to games nor telling stories. I personally believe that games have mountains of untapped potential when it comes to interactive storytelling; games can do things that no other medium can when it comes to immersing someone in a created world.
